IIT Delhi is a prestigious public technical and research institution in India, established in 1961 as a College of Engineering and declared an Institution of National Importance in 1963. The institute focuses on generating new knowledge through cutting-edge research and provides advanced academic programs across multiple disciplines, including engineering, physical sciences, and humanities. Situated in the historic Hauz Khas area of South Delhi, the 320-acre campus features a harmonious blend of architecture and natural beauty, with numerous buildings and scenic landscapes. A vast network of successful alumni contributes significantly to various sectors, including technology, business, public service, and entrepreneurship, with well-known figures like the founder of Zomato being alumni.
